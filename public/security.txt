# Antibioteka Security Policy

## Reporting Security Vulnerabilities

We take the security of our AI-powered antibiotic recommendation system seriously. 
Patient data protection and system integrity are our highest priorities.

### What to Report
- Authentication bypasses or privilege escalation
- Patient data exposure or HIPAA violations
- Clinical decision logic manipulation
- Database injection vulnerabilities
- Cross-site scripting (XSS) or CSRF issues
- Infrastructure security issues
- AI model poisoning or adversarial attacks

### What NOT to Report
- General medical device regulations (not applicable)
- Clinical outcome disputes (contact medical team)
- Billing or subscription issues (contact support)
- General software bugs (use our bug tracker)

### Response Timeline
- Acknowledgment: Within 24 hours
- Initial Assessment: Within 72 hours
- Resolution Target: 30 days for critical issues
- Public Disclosure: 90 days after fix (coordinated)

### Bug Bounty
We offer rewards for qualifying security reports:
- Critical: $1,000 - $5,000
- High: $500 - $1,000
- Medium: $100 - $500
- Low: Recognition and acknowledgment

### Legal
We commit to:
- No legal action for good faith security research
- Work with you on timeline for responsible disclosure
- Credit you in our security acknowledgments (optional)

### Compliance Framework
- HIPAA Security Rule compliance
- SOC 2 Type II certification
- ISO 27001 security standards
- Clinical system security guidelines
